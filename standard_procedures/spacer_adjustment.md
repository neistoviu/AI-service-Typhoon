# Standard Procedure: Spacer Adjustment and Plate Removal

> **When to use:** Gaps at rear of roaster, smoke from plate area, air leaks  
> ⚠️ Detailed instruction text to be added (referenced in cases as "instruction for adjusting and removing plates")

## Overview

Spacers are used to position the front part of the roaster. Over time or after seal changes, they may need to be removed and the front realigned.

## Steps

1. Identify the spacer locations
2. Remove spacers as needed
3. Realign front part of roaster (see [Front Alignment](front_alignment.md))
4. Check that plate sits flush — no gaps at:
   - Rear (most critical)
   - Sides
   - Bottom
5. Replace seals if deformed during the process
6. Verify with a test roast
