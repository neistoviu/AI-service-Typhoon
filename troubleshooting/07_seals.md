# 07 — Seals and Air Leaks

> **Total cases:** 6  
> **Frequency:** ★★★☆☆  
> **Affected models:** 10 electro, 20 electro, 5 kg

---

## Common Leak Locations

- **Neck** of roaster (seal)
- **Under the plate**
- **Between plate and chamber**
- **Rear** of roaster (spacers)
- **Jaw joints** at discharge
- **Heater door** (latches, hinges)
- **Under motor** (seal sags over time)

## Diagnostic Algorithm

1. **Visual inspection** of all seals
2. **Remove spacers** and realign front part (see [front alignment](../standard_procedures/front_alignment.md))
3. **Replace** deformed/compressed seals
4. **Tighten** latches and hinges

See also [Spacer Adjustment Procedure](../standard_procedures/spacer_adjustment.md)

## Solved Cases

| Client | Model | Details | Solution |
|--------|-------|---------|----------|
| Velvet coffee | 10 electro | Missing piece of seal under plate. Frequent deformation. Leak at rear. | Seal replacement + plate adjustment |
| Sam Yalla Coffee | 20 electro | Leak at rear + smoke from jaw joints at discharge | 1) Instructions for adjusting and removing plates. 2) Seal replacement. 3) Replace latches with longer ones (extended thread). |
| Michal Dvorak | 5 kg | Dirty mesh + severely collapsed seal under motor | Mesh cleaning + seal replacement under motor |
| Rösterei Cafeto | — | Peeling rubber | Seal replacement |

## Key Pattern

**Seal under motor** is a high-wear item across all models. It sags over time and causes:
- Air leaks → temperature loss
- False sensor readings
- Airflow reduction

Regular inspection recommended.

## Manual References

- Check if all parts of roaster are tightly closed and not leaking air: roasting chamber, chaff collector, doors, cyclone, motors
- Check all seals for damage and intact insulation
