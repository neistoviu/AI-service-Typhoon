# 16 — Cooling Table (Paddles)

> **Total cases:** 6  
> **Frequency:** ★☆☆☆☆  
> **Affected models:** 2.5 kg Cyclone, 5 kg, 10 electro

---

## Symptoms

- Cooling takes too long (e.g., 7 minutes instead of expected time)
- Paddle motor doesn't start
- Paddles slip / don't rotate properly
- Cone doesn't align with paddle shaft

## Solved Cases

| Client | Model | Details | Solution |
|--------|-------|---------|----------|
| Nommad Coffee | 2.5 kg Cyclone | Paddle motor burned | Motor replacement |
| Velvet coffee | 10 electro | Cone on plate doesn't align with paddle shaft | Cone adjustment / alignment |
| TRE-359 | 2.5 kg Cyclone | Damaged paddle gearbox | Gearbox replacement |

## Manual References

- Check no blockage in cooling table (remove and inspect)
- Check cooling pipe inside roaster and downstream for clogs
- Check cooling motor is working and its circuit breaker is on
