## ⚠️ FIRE RISK

### Daisuke Coffee (5 kg)
**Incident:** Condensate accumulated in exhaust pipe → caught fire → spread to filter → **serious damage.**

**Prevention:**
- Regular pipe cleaning
- Install inspection sections in exhaust piping
- Check for condensate accumulation regularly

## Key Insight

**Condensate management is critical for fire safety.** Exhaust piping must:
- Be as short as possible
- Have upward slope (minimum 15°)
- Include cleaning openings
- Include condensate drain valves
- Be inspected regularly
