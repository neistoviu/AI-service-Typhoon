# 05 — Smoke from Chamber / Plate

> **Total cases:** 7  
> **Frequency:** ★★★☆☆  
> **Affected models:** 5 kg, 20 electro

---

## Symptoms

- Smoke coming from plate area / back of roaster
- Smoke from tray side
- Smoke at certain ventilation positions but not others
- RoR drops accompanying smoke

## Diagnostic Algorithm

1. Check **seals under plate** and between plate and chamber
2. **Remove spacers** and realign front part of roaster (see [front alignment procedure](../standard_procedures/front_alignment.md))
3. Check **exhaust** — cleanliness, pipe length, draft
4. Check **ventilation handle position**

## Key Insight

On **Typhoon 20 electro**: smoke from plate after 3+ roasts is often caused by **heat deformation** of the plate. The plate may be slightly bent, creating gaps even with new seals. This is a known pattern.

## Manual References

- Check exhaust pipe opening regulator is functional
- Check no foreign objects in roasting chamber or heater chamber
- Check exhaust pipes inside roaster and downstream are not blocked
