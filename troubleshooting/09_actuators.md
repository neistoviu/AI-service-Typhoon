# 09 — Actuators

> **Total cases:** 5  
> **Frequency:** ★★☆☆☆  
> **Affected models:** 5 kg, 30 electro, 10 electro, 10 PRO

---

## Symptoms

- Actuator doesn't respond
- Actuator burned out (no visible cause)
- Jaw motor oscillates (goes back and forth)
- Exhaust valve too tight

## Diagnostic Algorithm

1. Check **slider groove** for contamination
2. Check **slider cleanliness**
3. If clean → pass to electrician for electrical diagnostics

## Solved Cases

| Client | Model | Details | Solution |
