# 11 — Electrical — Wires, Switches, Relays

> **Total cases:** 10  
> **Frequency:** ★★★☆☆  
> **Affected models:** 5 kg (wire burnout — model-specific!), 2.5 kg, 20 electro

---

## Symptoms

### Wire Burnout (5 kg specific!)
- Main switch doesn't turn on
- Burning smell from electrical cabinet
- Visible wire damage

### Relay Problems
- Intermittent power to heaters
- Wrong relay rating installed
- Solid-state relay shifted out of position

### Short Circuit
- Roaster shuts down mid-roast
- Breakers trip unexpectedly
