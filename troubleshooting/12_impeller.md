# 12 — Motor Impeller

> **Total cases:** 5  
> **Frequency:** ★★☆☆☆  
> **Affected models:** 5 kg, 2.5 kg Cyclone

---

## Symptoms

- Foreign sounds from motor area
- Scratches visible on scroll housing
- Impeller contacts the housing
- Bolts found inside housing

## Solution

All cases resolved by **impeller replacement**.

| Client | Model | Details | Solution |
|--------|-------|---------|----------|
| Michal Jakoubek | 2.5 kg Cyclone | Impeller contacts scroll housing | Impeller replacement / adjustment |
| Colombani ApS | 5 kg | Broken impeller | Impeller replacement |
| Nicki Sturrock | 5 kg | — | Impeller replacement |
| Nort van Hulten | 5 kg | — | Impeller replacement |
| Cubby House | 5 kg | Bolts fell out inside housing | Impeller replacement + bolt check |

## Note

If impeller damage is accompanied by motor vibration, also check:
- Seal under motor
- Bolt torque
- Hub condition

See also [Category 3 — Main Motor](03_main_motor.md)
