Commissioning Procedure for 2.5–5 kg Shop Roasters
English translation of the provided document
This document outlines the steps for remote commissioning and operator training for Typhoon shop roasters
with capacities of 2.5 kg and 5 kg.
1. Before the call
 Before the call with the client, check the phase sequence for the 4 motors on the roaster according to 
the manual (the manual will be uploaded in the service tasks branch).
 The client must complete the full check and provide video proof. Without video confirmation, we do not 
accept this step, in order to protect ourselves from future problems in case the client made a mistake 
during the check.
 An additional video manual will also be uploaded.
2. Before the call, send the client the electronic version of the roaster manual
 During the commissioning/training call, the following structure should be explained in advance:
2.1. Roaster operating principle based on the manual
2.2. Roasting theory
2.3. Artisan / Cropster connection
2.4. Preheating the roaster for 20 minutes
2.5. One joint roast
2.6. Client questions
2.7. Sending the training completion protocol for the client’s signature (clients often ignore this 
document; we need leverage for such cases — for example, if the protocol is not signed, roaster issues 
may not be treated as warranty cases, or a similar approach).
2.1. Roaster operating principle based on the manual
 The 5 kg roaster manual is used as the reference sample.
 Pages 4–5: roaster components. After every 5 roasts, remove chaff from the bucket and place the 
bucket back in position (check that it is tightly secured). Also explain the roaster ventilation control 
handle. Tighten the wires in the electrical cabinet once every six months.
 Page 12: roaster software.
 Page 14: profile table — it would be better to add a video here.
 Page 15: service mode section.
 Page 16: roaster buttons.
2.2. Roasting theory
 A short theory course for our shop roasters, covering time, temperature, and other roasting basics.
2.3. Connecting Artisan / Cropster
 Follow the steps below to connect Artisan to Typhoon:
1) Make sure your Artisan version is 2.8.4 or the latest available version.
2) Go to Config → Machine → Typhoon Roasters.
3) A popup window will appear where you need to select the roaster type (Shoproaster / Hybrid) and IP 
192.168.0.210.

Shoproaster — 2.5 kg, 5 kg
 Hybrid — 10 kg, 20 kg, 30 kg
4) Then select the energy type (gas / electric).
5) Select the roaster capacity (2.5 / 5 / 10 / 10 / 20 / 30).
6) Next, go to the adapter settings on your computer and configure the IP address and mask:
 IP: 192.168.0.211 or 192.168.0.12
 Mask: 255.255.255.0
7) Then restart the computer, as this is required by Artisan when changing the settings.
8) After restarting, make sure that the adapter settings have been saved on the computer.
Cropster help article: https://help.cropster.com/en/knowledge/typhoon-roasting-intelligence-ri-setup
2.4. Preheating the roaster for 20 minutes
 Start the roaster and preheat it for 20 minutes. During this time, the client prepares the green coffee for
roasting.
2.5. Joint roast
 After the client states what type of green coffee they want to roast, carry out one roast together. Before
starting, explain what buttons must be pressed and what needs to be opened at each step.
2.6. Client questions
 Answer the client’s questions, if any.
2.7. Training completion protocol
 After the equipment startup is completed, send the document to the client, and the client is required to 
sign it and return it.
