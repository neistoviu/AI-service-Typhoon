# Typhoon 20 kg Industrial Electric — Known Issues

## Top Issues (by frequency)

1. **Seals and smoke** — heat deformation of plate after 3+ roasts
2. **VFD** — short circuit, replacement needed
3. **Sensors (lower)** — gaps cause overheating errors
4. **Relay** — failures, incorrect ratings

## Model-Specific Issues

- **Smoke from plate after 3+ roasts:** Heat deformation warps the plate, creating gaps even with new seals. This is a known pattern for this model.
- **Plate may be bent** — standard seal replacement may not fully resolve smoke issue
- **Jaw motor over-closes** — sensors can be displaced → move sensor right + use tie rods
- **Power loss during roasting** — jaw motor tries to over-close when power returns
