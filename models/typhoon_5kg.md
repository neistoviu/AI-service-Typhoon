# Typhoon 5 kg / PRO — Known Issues

## Top Issues (by frequency)

1. **Heaters** — most affected model for heater burnout
2. **Wire burnout + main switch** — ⚠️ 5 kg-SPECIFIC recurring issue!
3. **Sensors** — upper/lower errors, temperature module burnout
4. **Temperature loss** — standard diagnostics
5. **Impeller** — replacement needed

## ⚠️ Model-Specific Critical Issue: Wire Burnout

**Wire burnout to the main switch is a RECURRING issue on Typhoon 5 kg.** Multiple cases (Dudes and Barbies, Good Times Coffee, Brassart Benjamin). Replacement is complex — requires wire routing through cabinet + possibly drilling additional hole.

## Other Model-Specific Issues

- **KM1 module:** Sometimes shipped as 18A instead of 25A — always verify rating
- **Condensate fire risk:** One serious case (Daisuke Coffee) — ensure regular pipe cleaning
- **Filter VFD:** Can burn after ~2 years of use
