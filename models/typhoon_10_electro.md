# Typhoon 10 kg Electro / PRO — Known Issues

## Top Issues (by frequency)

1. **Sensor divergence** — upper/lower readings don't match
2. **Seals** — air leaks, especially under plate
3. **VFD short circuit** — replacement needed
4. **Actuators** — loading actuator burns, exhaust valve too tight

## Model-Specific Issues

- **Upper/lower sensor divergence** — check seals and gaps first
- **Cone misalignment** with mixer shaft on cooling table
- **10 PRO variant:** Light dims at high temperatures (design limitation)
- **10 PRO variant:** Loading paddle can jam
- **Cyclone "explosions"** — actuator may close during roasting (potential design issue)
