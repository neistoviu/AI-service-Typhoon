# Typhoon 30 kg Electric — Known Issues

## Top Issues (by frequency)

1. **Sensors** — beans enter upper sensor opening (design issue)
2. **Actuators** — jaw motor oscillates
3. **Coffee remaining** in chamber on discharge

## Model-Specific Issues

- **Beans fly into upper sensor opening** — this is a **design issue** on the 30 electro. Solution: rivet a protective plate from the inside of the chamber to block the sensor opening from beans.
- **Jaw motor oscillates** (goes back and forth) — sometimes closing doesn't engage and chamber enters preheat while open. Currently unresolved — requires engineer investigation.
- **Coffee remains in chamber** during unloading — discharge diagnostics needed.
