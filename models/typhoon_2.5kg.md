# Typhoon 2.5 kg / Cyclone / PRO — Known Issues

> Model variants: 2.5 kg, 2.5 kg Cyclone, 2.5 kg PRO

## Top Issues (by frequency)

1. **Motor rattling / no signal** — seal under motor wears fast; VFD failures
2. **Heaters** — standard burnout, relay-related
3. **Sensors** — upper/lower sensor errors, spare sensors sometimes defective
4. **RCD tripping** — neutral wire on grounding bus (systemic factory issue)
5. **Impeller** — contacts scroll housing, replacement needed
6. **Airflow** — dirty mesh, incorrect phasing after power outages

## Model-Specific Issues

- **Seal under motor** wears faster than on larger models — inspect regularly
- **PLC no response** — restart procedure may resolve
- **Bearings fall from guides** — fixation needed
- **Bulb socket:** E26 base required
