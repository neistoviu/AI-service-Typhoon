WARNING!:
Disconnection and connection of the motor wiring is carried out first and is carried out only on
de-energized equipment.
Installation and dismantling of this element should be carried out by an EC&I engineer or an
electrician who has experience in connecting electric motors and working with 3-phase
connection.
To dismantle the motor with the impeller:
1 – dismantle 10 fastening nuts, Fig. 1, element 3.
2 – dismantle the decorative and protective motor linings. Fig. 1, element 3.
3 – dismantle 10 clamping nuts Fig. 1, element 2.
4 – dismantle the motor with the impeller, for this work 2 people are needed (motor is heavy!)
The weight of the structure is 35 - 50 kg. This work must be done carefully
so as not to damage the motor impeller and landing pins.
The motor must be installed in the reverse order of dismantlement. Tighten the fasteners until
the metal part stops against the seal. Avoid overtightening the fasteners as this can damage the
fixing studs!
During the first start after installing the motor, it is necessary to check the tightness of the
motor mount to the roster. If air suction appears in some areas, tighten the fasteners on them.
